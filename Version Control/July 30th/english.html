English

English is the study, use and enjoyment of the English language and its literature, delivered orally, visually and in writing, in a variety of forms for different purposes and audiences. Understanding how language works helps students make appropriate language choices and apply them in a variety of contexts. Students learn to analyze texts to understand the power of language to enrich and shape the lives of themselves and others. Students appreciate and enjoy texts in all formats. Studying the Aotearoa New Zealand Literature, Pacific Literature and World Literature helps students develop a sense of identity, an appreciation of Aotearoa New Zealand's unique heritage and an understanding of the world.
